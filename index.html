OUR CHURCH

Welcome to Salvation Church Of The Revival Of Faith, where the Spirit of God moves with power, purpose, and passion. We are not just a building — we are a body of believers driven by a divine mandate: to preach the Gospel, revive the broken, deliver the bound, and bring the lost into the saving grace of Jesus Christ.
In a world filled with confusion, pain, and spiritual dryness, we stand as a beacon of truth, hope, and restoration. We believe in the power of revival — a fresh outpouring of the Holy Spirit that awakens hearts, heals wounds, and reignites purpose.
Our mission is simple but urgent:
To save souls, to set captives free, and to build up a generation rooted in faith and empowered by the Word.
Every message preached, every hand lifted, every prayer whispered in our sanctuary is centered on Jesus — the Author and Finisher of our faith. Through the power of the Holy Spirit, we see chains broken, lives transformed, and destinies restored.
So whether you're seeking healing, restoration, or a deeper relationship with God — there is a place for you here.
Come. Be renewed. Be revived. Be free.
Welcome to Salvation Church Of The Revival Of Faith — where souls are saved, lives are changed, and Jesus is Lord.
